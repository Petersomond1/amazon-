Users: Stores user information.
Categories: Stores product categories.
Products: Stores product information and is linked to categories.
Orders: Stores order information and is linked to users.
Order Items: Stores items within an order and is linked to orders and products.
Cart Items: Stores items within a user's cart and is linked to users and products.
Payments: Stores payment information for orders and is linked to orders.
Home Page Content: Stores dynamic content for the home page managed by the admin.
